A dedicated filter pitcher or faucet mount is the most affordable way to improve your drinking water without the waste of bottled water. When choosing, consider your space, flow needs, and the contaminants you most want to reduce. Pitchers are versatile but slower; faucet mounts save counter space but require compatible hardware.
