How we choose our picks

We don’t run our own lab tests or time filtration speeds with a stopwatch. Instead, we base every pick on a careful synthesis of three sources: the reputations of each brand among water-treatment experts, hundreds of long-term owner reviews (especially those written after six months of use), and published evaluations from established consumer-research organizations. We pay close attention to what actually goes wrong over time, things like lids that crack, filters that clog prematurely, or a plastic taste that develops after a few cycles. We also track the real cost of ownership. A filter may be cheap upfront but expensive if you have to replace cartridges every few weeks. By focusing on durability, filter availability, and verified contaminant reduction, we aim to recommend products that will serve you well for years, not just weeks after unboxing.
